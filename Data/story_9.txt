On the edge of the universe, explorers discovered a doorway to another reality. In the distant future, humans had colonized the stars, but they were not alone in the universe. As the last leaf fell from the ancient tree, a prophecy long forgotten was set into motion. In the quiet solitude of the desert, a traveler discovered a secret oasis guarded by ancient spirits. A mysterious illness spread through the town, but one girl seemed to hold the key to the cure.
