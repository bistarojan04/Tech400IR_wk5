In a small village at the foot of the mountains, legends spoke of a hidden treasure. As the last leaf fell from the ancient tree, a prophecy long forgotten was set into motion. A mysterious illness spread through the town, but one girl seemed to hold the key to the cure. A scientist working late into the night discovered something that would change the course of history. In the quiet solitude of the desert, a traveler discovered a secret oasis guarded by ancient spirits. On the edge of the universe, explorers discovered a doorway to another reality.
