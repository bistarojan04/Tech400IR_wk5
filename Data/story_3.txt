The machine that could rewrite memories was both a blessing and a curse, as people soon learned. In a small village at the foot of the mountains, legends spoke of a hidden treasure. A forgotten map found in an old library led them on a quest for an artifact of great power. The explorers set sail on a vast ocean, chasing the horizon as it melted into the sky. On the edge of the universe, explorers discovered a doorway to another reality. A scientist working late into the night discovered something that would change the course of history.
