In the quiet solitude of the desert, a traveler discovered a secret oasis guarded by ancient spirits. The city beneath the sea was a marvel of human achievement, but it held dark secrets within. As the last leaf fell from the ancient tree, a prophecy long forgotten was set into motion. A forgotten map found in an old library led them on a quest for an artifact of great power. In a small village at the foot of the mountains, legends spoke of a hidden treasure. The kingdom was in turmoil, but a stranger from a distant land brought hope where there was none. In the distant future, humans had colonized the stars, but they were not alone in the universe.
